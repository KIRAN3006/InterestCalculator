Simple Intrest

Simple interest is a type of interest that is calculated as a
percentage of the principal amount borrowed or invested over
a certain period of time. It is a straightforward method of calculating interest that is
commonly used in a variety of financial transactions,
such as loans, bonds, and savings accounts. The formula for simple
interest is relatively simple, as it only takes into account the
principal amount, the interest rate, and the time period.

Formula to calculate Simple Intrest:

    S I = P * T * R

            Where,
P = Loan amount (principal)
R = Monthly interest rate (annual interest rate divided by 12)
T = Number of months in the loan tenure
