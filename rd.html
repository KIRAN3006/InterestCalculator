Recurring deposit

R D (Recurring Deposit) is a financial product offered by banks
and financial institutions that allows individuals
to save a fixed amount of money at regular intervals,
typically on a monthly basis.
it is a popular investment option for individuals who
want to cultivate a disciplined savings habit and earn
fixed return on their savings.
The formula to calculate RD is:

    
        M = P * [ (1+r/n)^(n*t)-1]                                         
            ------------------
              (1-(1+r/n)^(-1/n))                    
        
            Where,
P = Loan amount (principal)
r = Monthly interest rate (annual interest rate divided by 12)
n = Number of months in the loan tenure
